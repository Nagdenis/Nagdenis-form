UK Scholarship Application Form For Refugees In Kakuma, Kenya

Welcome to the UK Scholarship Application for Refugees in Kakuma, Kenya. This scholarship aims to support talented and driven individuals residing in the Kakuma Refugee Camp who seek to further their education and enhance their future prospects. We understand the unique challenges faced by refugees and are committed to providing opportunities that can help transform lives and build a brighter future.
